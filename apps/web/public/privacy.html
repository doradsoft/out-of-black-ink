Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 17, 2026

out-of-black-ink converts black and gray PDF content to color ink. The public web app performs conversion in your browser.

Files

PDFs selected in the browser app are processed locally in your browser. They are not uploaded to the out-of-black-ink GitHub Pages site or Cloudflare Worker.

ChatGPT App

The ChatGPT App endpoint currently opens the browser-based converter and serves app metadata. It does not process, store, or transmit PDF files.

Logs

Hosting providers such as GitHub Pages and Cloudflare may collect standard operational logs such as request time, IP address, user agent, and requested URL.

Contact

For privacy questions, open an issue at GitHub Issues.
