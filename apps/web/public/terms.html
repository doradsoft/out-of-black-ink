out-of-black-ink helps recolor black and gray PDF content for printing. The public web app runs conversion in your browser.

You are responsible for the files you choose to process and for checking the converted output before printing or sharing it.

Files and output

The browser app processes selected PDFs locally in your browser. The ChatGPT App endpoint currently opens the browser-based converter and does not process or store PDF files.
